---

## Project 1 — Henry Hub Price Analyzer

**File:** `price_analyzer.py`

A pure-Python script that analyzes a series of Henry Hub 
natural gas spot prices with no external libraries.

**Outputs:**
- Current price, average, high, low
- Period price change ($ and %)
- Day-over-day moves
- Market tone (bullish / bearish / flat)

**Concepts covered:** lists, loops, list comprehensions, 
conditionals, f-strings, floating point handling

**Why it matters:** Henry Hub is the primary pricing 
benchmark for US natural gas. Understanding price movement, 
range, and tone is the foundation of any nat gas market 
analysis workflow.
